📄 Terms & Conditions — Please Read Before Submitting ▼
1. Nature of Submission
This form is a needs assessment only. Submission does not constitute a service booking, purchase order, work order, or any guarantee of service delivery by KLE-CTiE or EE Lab.
2. Facility Development Status
The EE Lab SMT assembly facility at KLE-CTiE is currently under procurement and development. Equipment availability, operational readiness, and service commencement are subject to institutional procurement timelines and management approvals.
3. Service Availability Timeline
Basic reflow assembly capability is targeted for Q3 2026 (September). Pick-and-place capability is planned for Q4 2026. These timelines are indicative and subject to change without prior notice. KLE-CTiE bears no liability for delays.
4. No Pricing Commitment
No pricing, quotation, or cost estimate implied or stated in this form constitutes a binding offer. Final service charges will be communicated separately and officially upon facility readiness and management approval.
5. Turnaround Time
No turnaround time commitments can be made at this stage. ASAP or urgent requests noted in this assessment are for planning purposes only and cannot be guaranteed. All turnaround commitments, if any, will be made in writing only after facility operational readiness is confirmed.
6. Confidentiality of Design Files
Design files (Gerber, BOM, CPL, schematics) shared subsequently with the EE Lab will be treated as confidential and proprietary to the submitter. Files will be used solely for assessment and assembly purposes and will not be shared with any third party without explicit written consent of the submitter.
7. Acknowledgement of Assessment
You will receive an email acknowledgement within 1 working day of submission. This acknowledgement confirms receipt of your assessment only — it does not constitute a service agreement, work order, or any form of commitment by KLE-CTiE.
8. Contact & Grievances
